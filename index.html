Impostor

The aim of “Find The Impostor is to expose a secret impostor in the group. All players are given a secret role: most are normal players, but one or more participants are impostors. A random word is chosen and each player must name a word for the word. The impostor must try to guess the word, while the innocents try to guess the impostor.

#game #groupgame #multiplayer
