        What is N'Track?

        N'Track is a simple, modern, reliable and completely free grade tracker to give you a better overview of your grades and show you which subjects you should try harder in.
        All this, without ads and without premium features, because the app is already ‚ú®PREMIUM ‚ú® enough and we think that everyone should be able to monitor their grades well and easily without having to spend money on it.
