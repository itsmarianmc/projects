How to play

Cookie Clicker is a popular and well-known game in which you receive a cookie when you press a cookie. With the cookies you earn, you can buy upgrades and automatic production buildings such as grannies or robots to generate cookies even faster.
What do the respective upgrades do?
  • Handschuhe: Your collected cookies, upgrades and other game progress are saved in your browser. This means that the next time you load the page, you can continue where you left off without losing your progress.
  • Cookie Robot: A cookie robot gives you 1 automatic cookie per second
  • Granny: A granny gives you 5 automatic cookies per second, i.e. 4 more than a robot and is therefore more expensive. The more grannies you have, the more automatic income you have!
  • Prestige: Prestige resets your current progress and gives you a prestige star. However, it increases your global income by 1.5. The higher the number of prestige stars, the better your cookie. When you have 20 prestige stars, the game is over.
