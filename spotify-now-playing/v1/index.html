Spotify Now Playing Widget

If you are using OBS to implement this widget, go to the "Sources" tab, click the browser source, right click and click "Interact" to authorize!


Click on "Authorize" and then on agree. After that you will be redirected to this site.

You will see the widget 10 seconds after the start of a song and 22 seconds before the end of a song.
